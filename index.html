About Kranti

A virtual technical symposium for the year 2021, organised by the the students of Meenakshi sundararajan Engineering college's Computer Science Engineering department. It is a series of technical events conducted for the uprooting Tech-savy technologists . It has always been incredibly popular over the years and has entertained and enriched Students with great knowledge!

Kranti 2k21 s conducted exceptionally on a virtual platform. So come join us and integrate your intelligence with technology.

Mark the date : 10th April 2021.
